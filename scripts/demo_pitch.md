# Judge Pitch Script

## Opening
We are solving one of the biggest hidden problems in sports media: official assets spread across the internet faster than media teams can verify, monitor, or enforce ownership.

## What makes our solution special
Most tools either watermark content or detect duplicates. We do both — and we add explainable incident intelligence.

## Demo flow
1. Register an official sports poster.
2. The system generates exact hash, perceptual fingerprints, semantic signature, and provenance manifest.
3. Upload a cropped or edited repost as suspicious evidence.
4. The system compares it across multiple signals.
5. It produces a confidence score, likely transformations, and an action recommendation.
6. Benchmark endpoint demonstrates robustness under crop, blur, resize, and overlay.

## Closing line
We are not just tracking copies. We are authenticating source media, reconstructing suspicious propagation, and making enforcement easier.
